Design Edicts
  • Figure out what fun is and make it rewarding.
  • Make the fun competitive.
  • No Fetch/FedEx Quests
  • Have lots of guns and even more bullets.
  • Rewards need to drop during that activity that awarded them.

  • a weapon that shoots bullets
  • hit scan bullets
  • traveling bullets

  • a few sounds
For the prototype, I'm using sprite resources from Kenney Assets.

I also added a couple of design edicts to the list (see top of page).
